THE AEGEAN

Welcome to the world of ancient Greece.

A fantasy massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) deeply rooted in history and mythology, published and developed by Leonidarx.

Live your own epic journey as a citizen of the Ancient World, where you define your own path. Join one of the many states, such as Sparta or Macedonia, or choose to fight against all Greeks by joining the Persian Empire. Each with their own culture and equipment, where like-minded players fight for survival. It is your choice whether you want to participate in combat, or dedicate to one of the many trades, selling your products to other players and helping each other achieve specific goals. Take a step further, and join player-made Armies as a leader, warrior, or supplier. Choose the lone path, fighting mythological creatures for treasures, or do it all. The choice is yours.

The Aegean Economy

The Aegean economy reflects that of real life. We have chosen to leave it up to players to decide based on supply and demand. Trading may occur anywhere along the vast world of The Aegean, but players dedicated to the art of marchantilism may want to head over to dedicated markets where they'll find the bulk of buyers looking for rare items or simple supplies.

Resources, Supplies, and Equipment

Every resource, piece of armor, or weapon is made or acquired by you, the player, assuming you have the skill needed.

Q: How do I get my first iron armor set?

Find an iron deposit, mine it, and smith it yourself, or buy it from another player.

Q: Food replenishes my Hitpoints. How do I get food?

Go hunting, fishing, bake your own food, or buy it from another player.

Q: I want my first bronze short sword! How do I get one?

This is called the 'Bronze Xiphos', made by player smiths with enough skill, by mining tin and copper, then smelting them into a bronze bar. Buy it from another player, or make it from scratch.

Q: How do I get the rare and powerful bow, Toxa?

The 'Toxa Bow' is acquired by killing the powerful Minotaur, in the island of Crete. Step up to the challenge, or buy it from someone who did.

Our Progression System

Experience is mostly obtained by performing tasks related to a specific skill, but also questing, random events, minigames, or achievements. After gaining a certain amount of experience, players will advance to the next level in that skill, which unlocks new abilities and areas around the world.

Every players starts at level 1 in every skill except for Hitpoints at 10. Every skill can reach the maximum level of 100, a massive achievement. You will find that the first levels are quickly obtained, but as you progress, it becomes exponentially more difficult and delayed. Only the most dedicated players will ever max a skill. They will be rewarded.

Our Goal

The Aegean is a game where you can choose how you play. Whether you are looking for a challenge or to relax, we want you to feel rewarded. The most challenging events will make you sweat, guaranteed. But those who want a more relaxing experience also have their place. Our economy makes sure of that. You can obtain almost any item in the game, whether by hard work or by trading. All types of players have their place in The Aegean.
